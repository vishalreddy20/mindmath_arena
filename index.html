How To Play

You are doing number puzzles with bubbles.

  1. Tap a number bubble to split it into smaller bubbles.
  2. Pick an operator first, then type numbers that make the same value.
  3. Drag one number bubble onto another to combine and make the final answer.
Example: 14 can be 10 + 4 or 7 + 7.
